# Product Brief: Ai assistant

## Executive Summary

Ai assistant is a high-agency, autonomous AI Executive Assistant designed specifically for CEOs and leaders of small enterprises who lack extensive administrative support. Unlike traditional scheduling tools that merely manage time, Ai assistant acts as a proactive "Proxy Agent," handling the cognitive load of follow-ups ("relancing"), information classification, and autonomous task execution. By reclaiming at least 3 hours of high-value leadership time per week, it allows CEOs to focus on strategic growth rather than administrative logistics.

---

## Core Vision

### Problem Statement

CEOs in small-to-medium enterprises are overwhelmed by "administrative friction"—the constant need to schedule meetings, filter emails, set reminders, and manually follow up on delegated tasks. This cognitive load leads to bottlenecks where projects stall because a leader forgot to "nudge" a contributor or couldn't respond to a routine information request in time.

### Problem Impact

When a CEO fails to follow up (the "relancing" gap), projects drift, client demands for information go unanswered, and team momentum dies. The manual handling of these tasks currently costs leaders several hours of peak productivity per week and increases the risk of critical information being lost in the noise of messages and emails.

### Why Existing Solutions Fall Short

Current "AI Assistants" (like Motion or Reclaim) are largely reactive schedulers. They tell you *when* to work but don't *do* the work. Human secretaries are effective but expensive and not always available 24/7. Existing tools fail to:
1. Proactively "nudge" or follow up with team members to ensure task completion.
2. Act as a proxy for the CEO, executing routine tasks or providing information autonomously based on pre-approved rules and historical context.

### Proposed Solution

An AI-driven Agent that serves as a digital "Chief of Staff." It intelligently classifies all incoming communication, manages calendar conflicts with executive-level reasoning, and—most importantly—proactively follows up on every delegated task or client inquiry. It has the agency to execute pre-authorized actions in the CEO's absence, ensuring that business momentum never stops.

### Key Differentiators

- **Proactive Accountability ("Relancing"):** Automatically chases updates and follows up with people on the CEO's behalf without being prompted.
- **Autonomous Proxy Execution:** Can perform routine tasks and respond to information requests in the CEO's absence based on a library of "allowed actions" and historical patterns.
- **Outcome-Driven, Not Just Time-Driven:** Focuses on moving tasks from "pending" to "complete" rather than just moving blocks on a calendar.
- **High-Agency "Brain":** Uses LLM-based reasoning to understand the nuances of executive communication and decision-making.

## Target Users

### Primary Users

**The "Overstretched CEO" (e.g., Sarah)**
- **Profile:** Leaders of small/medium enterprises (10-50 employees) without a full-time Chief of Staff.
- **Motivations:** Reclaiming time, reducing cognitive load, and maintaining business momentum with absolute control.
- **Key Use Case:** 
    - **High-Agency Proxy:** Autonomous responses on "Allowed Topics" with configurable confidence thresholds.
    - **Agency Perimeter Control:** Real-time monitoring and "Emergency Brake" for all proxy actions.

### Secondary Users

**The "Scaling Project Manager" (e.g., Marcus)**
- **Profile:** PMs or Department Heads responsible for execution and reporting.
- **Motivations:** Automating status-chasing while maintaining team morale.
- **Key Use Case:** 
    - **Context-Aware Relancing:** Nudging team members based on their availability and focus hours.
    - **Smart Escalation:** Automatically flagging blocked tasks to a human when AI nudges fail.

### User Journey

1. **Discovery & Setup:** User integrates channels and defines "Agency Tiers" (Public, Controlled, Restricted).
2. **Autonomous Triage:** AI classifies mail, handling Tier 1 (Public) automatically and drafting Tier 2 (Controlled) for approval.
3. **The "Relancing" Loop:** AI proactively nudges team members, respecting their "Focus Time" and OOO status.
4. **The "Aha!" Moment:** A routine request is handled perfectly by the AI, and the CEO receives a "Morning Brief" showing 3 hours of work saved.
5. **The Feedback Loop:** CEO reviews AI actions, providing "Corrective Feedback" that immediately updates the AI's future reasoning patterns.

## Success Metrics

Success for Ai assistant is measured by the degree to which it becomes an invisible but indispensable extension of the leader's agency.

### User Success
- **Autonomy Volume:** Number of messages/emails handled entirely as a Proxy without human intervention.
- **Logistical Velocity:** Time from a "High-Level Command" (e.g., "Plan a meeting ASAP") to execution completion.
- **Reclaimed Focus:** Total hours per week the CEO spends on strategic growth vs. administrative logistics.

### Business Objectives
- **3-Month Goal:** Establish a "Trust Foundation" where 60% of routine communication is handled autonomously with <5% error rate.
- **12-Month Goal:** Become the standard "Digital Chief of Staff" for the SME market, targeting a 90% retention rate through demonstrable time savings.

### Key Performance Indicators
- **Proxy Success Rate:** % of "Allowed Topic" requests resolved without human take-over.
- **Scheduling Precision:** % of meeting orders executed correctly on the first attempt without manual calendar correction.
- **"Relancing" Impact:** % reduction in project bottlenecks caused by missing follow-ups.

## MVP Scope

### Core Features
- **Primary Integration:** Full Google Workspace support (Gmail & Calendar).
- **Messaging Proxy:** Integration with WhatsApp/Telegram for mobile-first leadership.
- **Logistical Autopilot:** Natural language meeting planning and scheduling.
- **"Relancing" (Proactive Follow-up):** Automated status chasing and notification of task completion.
- **Employee Knowledge Base:** Storage of roles and responsibilities to provide context for proxy actions.

### Out of Scope for MVP
- **Voice & Telephony:** No voice-based interaction or call handling.
- **Financial Autonomy:** No budgeting, invoicing, or financial reporting.
- **Deep Integration:** No custom integrations with niche enterprise tools (ERP/CRM).

### MVP Success Criteria
- **Execution Precision:** 90%+ success rate on "Plan Meeting" commands.
- **Categorization Accuracy:** Zero "Restricted Topic" leaks (100% accuracy on Agency Perimeter).
- **User Value:** Minimum 2 hours of high-value time reclaimed per week for pilot users.

### Future Vision
- **Autonomous SME:** A self-coordinating enterprise where the AI manages the baseline operational momentum, leaving humans to focus entirely on creative and strategic growth.
